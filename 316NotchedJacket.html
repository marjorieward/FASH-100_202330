  • Assignment phases:
    • Phase 1: Research and development
      • Students will develop and design a structured blazer/jacket.
      • Students will source the necessary underpinnings for their blazer/jacket design (i.e. shoulder pads).
      • Students will drape, true, balance, and pin-proof their blazer/jacket design on an industry standard dressform.
      • Blazer/Jacket designs MUST exhibit a lapel and collar.
      • Utilize creative problem solving given the scope of the course and the amount of time alloted for the assignment.
    • Phase 2: Testing, Drafting and Construction
      • Students will test elements of their design construction when necessary.
      • Finalize trued drapes and transfer to alphanumeric pattern paper.
      • Students will derive facing and lining patterns from their trued and transferred drapes.
    • Phase 3: Final Construction
      • Students will cut their patterns from chosen fabrics and assemble into a final garment.
    • Garment must have a NOTCHED COLLAR (i.e. lapel and collar)
