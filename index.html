Class One - Introduction to Sewing

✕

Before Class:

  • Complete the Pre-Quarter Assignment
  • Watch the Hand-Sewing Tutorial on Blackboard
  • Bring the following pattern pieces (cut, notched, and marks transferred) to class:
    • Hand Sewing Practice Pattern (1 piece)
  • Bring the following materials to class:
    • Laptop or Tablet
    • Hand Sewing Needles
    • All-purpose thread in a DARK color
    • Medium weight muslin
    • Clear ruler with 1/8 inch markings
    • Frixion brand pen(s)
    • Fabric Scissors
    • Transfer Paper
    • Serrated Transfer Wheel
    • Tape Measure
    • Thread snips/embroidery scissors (optional)

During Class:

  • Welcome to the Sewing Studio
  • Hand Sewing stitches: running stitch (basting) and hand overcast (finishing edges)

After Class:

  • Complete Hand Sewing sample
  • Assignment 1 – Sewing Machine Operation Test on Blackboard due by end of Week One.
