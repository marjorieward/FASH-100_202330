  • Assignment phases:
    • Phase 1: Research and development
      • Students will conduct extensive research of their assigned runway look to determine its construction.
      • Consider researching images or photos of the runway look.
      • Consider researching the designer and other garments they may have fabricated.
      • Utilize creative problem solving for any context/construction undetermined by factual research.
      • Utilize creative problem solving given the scope of the course and the amount of time alloted for the assignment.
    • Phase 2: Testing and Prototyping
      • Students will acquire fabric swatches to analyze their construction and drape and compare the testing to the originally assigned look.
      • Chosen fashion fabric must resemble the original runway look in weight and drape ONLY.
      • Students will test their initial patterns with garment prototypes and make changes to their patterns if need be prior to final construction.
      • Practice construction or finishing techniques prior to making the final garment.
    • Phase 3: Drafting and Construction
      • Students will finalize their patterns, then cut and construction their garment from final fashion fabric.
